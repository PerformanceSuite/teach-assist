## FAQ (Copy-Paste Responses)

### Q: "How much does it cost?"

The software is free (open source). There is a small API cost for the AI (Claude), which we estimate at $5-20/month for typical pilot usage. **We'll cover your first $50**, so cost shouldn't be a barrier during the pilot.

### Q: "What if I'm not very tech-savvy?"

If you can install apps on your computer and navigate a web browser, you'll be fine! The setup guide is step-by-step (15 minutes), and we'll provide support. Several pilot teachers have said the setup was easier than expected.

### Q: "Can I use it with my students?"

Not yet! v0.1 is for *teacher* use only (planning, curriculum work, professional tasks). **Never upload student work or PII.** Future versions may include student-facing features, but that's not part of this pilot.

### Q: "What if it doesn't work or I don't like it?"

Totally fine! We want honest feedback. If something's broken, confusing, or not useful, that's exactly what we need to know. You can stop participating anytime, no questions asked.

### Q: "Will this replace teachers?"

Absolutely not. TeachAssist is built on the principle that teachers are irreplaceable. AI assists, you decide. We're teachers ourselves and deeply believe in teacher judgment.

### Q: "How do I know my data is safe?"

Everything runs locally on your computer. Your documents never leave your machine. The only thing sent to the AI service is your questions + relevant excerpts (not full documents). We never see your data. Full details in the setup guide.

### Q: "What subjects/grades does it work with?"

All subjects and grades! We've designed it to be curriculum-agnostic. Pilot teachers span elementary to high school, across STEM, humanities, arts, and more.

### Q: "I have a Mac/Windows - will it work?"

Yes! The setup guide covers both Mac and Windows with step-by-step instructions for each.

### Q: "Can I share this with other teachers?"

Please do! We'd love referrals to teachers who'd give thoughtful feedback. Just forward the email or have them reach out directly.
